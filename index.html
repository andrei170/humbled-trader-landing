FAQ

Common Questions

How much does it cost?

The Academy is application based and pricing is discussed on the onboarding webinar once we've confirmed fit. Payment plans are available for accepted applicants. I deliberately don't list a hard price on the page because the decision should come after you've seen what's inside, not before.

How much trading capital do I need to start?

Minimum $2,000 to apply. Realistically $5,000 is a better starting point because it gives you enough runway to take reps at one percent risk per trade without running out of capital during the learning phase.

Do you give signals or alerts?

No. This is the opposite of a signal service. The whole point of the Academy is to teach you to build your own rules and trade your own conviction. Signals rent you somebody else's confidence. That's not a skill.

How long before I'm profitable?

Most consistent members hit their first profitable quarter somewhere in month 6 to 9. Some faster. Some slower. Anyone who promises you a timeline in this skill is selling you something. The one thing I'll commit to is that if you actually follow the four habits for 12 straight months, you'll know whether this career is for you or not.

Who should not apply?

Anyone looking for a hot pick next week. Anyone expecting to quit their job in 30 days. Anyone who isn't willing to journal every trade for at least six months. And anyone with less than $2,000 in trading capital. If that's you, watch the free YouTube content and come back when the timing is right.
